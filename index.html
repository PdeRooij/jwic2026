What's in the bag?

Magnet

Typically Dutch is a so-called "tegeltjeswijsheid" (translated literally: tile wisdom). A memorable, wise or witty saying that is sometimes printed on a tile to hang on or put into a wall. Usually a tile like that is produced as Delft Blue (Dutch: Delfts blauw). This magnet resembles a tegeltjeswijsheid. It is based on Proverbs 26:20. Unfortunately it is not an authentic Delft Blue tile, but a magnet is more convenient and durable (and also a lot cheaper ;)).
